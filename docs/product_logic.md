# Product Logic - InsightSync

## 1. Input Layer

* Company reports
* Financial statements
* Market intelligence (news, industry, policy)

---

## 2. Processing Layer

### Step 1: Signal Extraction

* Revenue, margin, growth
* External events (policy, competition)

### Step 2: Interpretation (LLM)

* Explain why signal matters

### Step 3: Business Mapping

* Translate into business impact

### Step 4: RM Action Mapping

* Link to banking products:

  * Credit
  * Trade finance
  * FX
  * Cash management

---

## 3. Output Layer

Each insight includes:

* Signal
* Interpretation
* Business Impact
* RM Action
* Source

---

## 4. Design Principles

* Explainable (traceable source)
* Actionable (RM can use directly)
* Structured (consistent format)
* Business-oriented (not just analysis)

---

## 5. Key Differentiation

InsightSync is NOT a summarization tool.

It is a:
→ Decision Support Tool for Relationship Managers
→ Revenue-enabling intelligence system
