# InsightSync Demo

## 🔍 What is InsightSync

InsightSync is an AI-powered client intelligence briefing assistant designed for Relationship Managers (RMs) in commercial banking.

It transforms fragmented company reports and external market intelligence into **actionable, traceable, and banker-friendly insights**.

---

## 🎯 Problem

RMs spend significant time reviewing:

* Company earnings reports
* Financial news
* Industry developments

However, information is **fragmented and not directly actionable**.

---

## 💡 Solution

InsightSync generates structured insights by combining:

* Financial signals
* Market context
* Business implications
* Banking opportunities

---

## ⚙️ Demo Flow

1. **Input**

   * Company earnings reports
   * External market intelligence

2. **Processing**

   * Step 1: Extract structured signals
   * Step 2: LLM generates interpretation
   * Step 3: Attach source reference
   * Step 4: Map to banking opportunity

3. **Output**

   * Insight
   * Explanation
   * Source
   * RM Action

---

## 📊 Example Insight

**Revenue declined 8% YoY (source: earnings report), indicating weak global PC demand.**
→ Potential working capital pressure

**RM Action:**

* Explore working capital financing
* Offer supply chain financing solutions

---

## 🚀 Value for Banking

* Enable more professional, data-backed conversations
